Our Story

Top Tier! Kasi Burgers...

The journey began in 2018 right in the heart of Kagiso Township. It was sparked by the founders love for burgers and hopes to pursue a career in hospitality.

Mookie started the business from scratch in his home, those were truly humble beginnings but this did not stop him. He gradually improved over the years; now with a fully functioning kitchen and seating area for customers which was proudly sponsored by Coca-Cola.

Their target market is all demographics from residential areas, schools and the industrial Chamdor area located in Kagiso. Mookie's Munchiies has grown impressively that they've sponsored events hosted by NPO's and collaborated with upcoming local brands such as 'Waya-Waya'
